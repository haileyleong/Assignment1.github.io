Discover East Coast Park

Looking for a place to chill and have fun? Well, look no further! Embrace nature and drown yourself in the many fun activities at ECP. 

Directions Find out more

Background

Founded in 1970, East Coast Park is home to 15 kilometres stretch of beaches and is widely popular among beach lovers, friends and families. With many activities catering to people of all ages, it is a common destination for the locals. You'll never be bored at ECP!
