Discover East Coast Park

Embrace nature and drown yourself in the many fun activities at ECP!


Background


Founded in 1970, East Coast Park is home to 15 kilometres stretch of beaches and is widely popular among beach lovers, friends and families. With many activities catering to people of all ages, it is a common destination for locals. You'll never be bored at ECP!
