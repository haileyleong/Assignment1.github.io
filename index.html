Discover East Coast Park

Embrace nature and drown yourself in the many fun activities at ECP

Founded in 1970, East Coast Park also known as "ECP" is home to 15 kilometres
stretch of beaches and is widely popular among beach lovers, friends and families.

With many activities catering to people of all ages, it is a common destination for locals.

You'll never be bored at ECP!

    Get Active!



    		 Aloha Sea Sport Centre

    No better place to attempt Laser Sailing, Stand Up Paddling, Windfoiling and Windsurfing!

    Family-friendly courses for all ages are provided and don't worry about the equipment as there is a rental service available.

    After an eventful session out at sea, you can even drop by their very own beach bar and cafe to fill your stomachs.

    Find out more from here.

    Cable Ski

    Try cable skiing at Singapore Wake Park - Singapore's one and only cable-ski park!

    People of different skill levels are welcomed! The park has three cable systems to cater to beginners as well as experienced riders, providing all sort of challenges for riders.

    Book your ride now!


    		Cyclist Park

    Enjoy cycling?

    Why not cycle in ECP's wonderful nature and challenge youself at the cyclist park! It's definitely a hassle to bring your own bike all the way to ECP, so fret not, as ECP has its very own bike rental service.

    Click here to find out where you can rent a bike.

    Xtreme Skatepark

    The biggest vertical bowl we have in Singapore! The east coast park Xtreme skatepark is located at F1 Carpark at East Coast Park.

    Calling all skateboarders! Challenge yourself at Xtreme Skatepark.


    		Volleyball

    Nothing beats a fun game of volleyball with your friends on the beach!

    Bring your family and friends to ECP now and have some fun under the sun!
